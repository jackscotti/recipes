# Cold Spicy Tahini Udon

A no-cook-sauce, refreshing summer noodle bowl: cold udon in a creamy, tangy
tahini-chili dressing. About 15 minutes.

**Prep time:** 10 minutes
**Cook time:** 5 minutes
**Servings:** 2

---

## Ingredients

**Tahini dressing**
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp chili crisp, plus extra to serve
- 1 tsp maple syrup or honey
- 1 clove garlic, grated
- Cold water, to loosen

**To serve**
- 2 portions udon noodles
- 1/2 cucumber, julienned
- 2 scallions, sliced
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Soft-boiled egg or edamame (optional)

---

## Method

1. Whisk the dressing ingredients together, adding cold water a little at a time
   until smooth and pourable.

2. Cook the udon per the package instructions, then rinse thoroughly under cold
   water and drain well.

3. Toss the cold noodles with the dressing. Top with cucumber, scallions, sesame
   seeds, and egg or edamame if using.

4. Finish with an extra spoonful of chili crisp.

---

## Notes

- Best eaten straight away while cold and fresh.
- Works with soba too, for a nuttier, lighter bowl.
