# Carrot Cake Recipe
*Healthier version: oil replaced with Greek yogurt, reduced sugar, metric measurements*

**Servings:** 18  
**Prep Time:** 30 mins  
**Cook Time:** 40 mins  
**Total Time:** 1 hr 20 mins  

## Cake Ingredients:

- **250g** white sugar *(reduced from original 400g)*
- **175g** plain Greek yogurt *(replaces 1¼ cups vegetable oil)*
- **4** large eggs
- **10ml** vanilla extract
- **250g** all-purpose flour
- **10g** baking soda
- **8g** baking powder
- **6g** ground cinnamon
- **3g** salt
- **300g** grated carrots
- **110g** chopped pecans *(optional)*

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare:** Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease and flour a 23x33cm pan.

2. **Make batter:** Beat sugar, Greek yogurt, eggs, and 10ml vanilla together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until well combined.

3. **Add dry ingredients:** Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in carrots, then fold in pecans.

4. **Bake:** Pour into prepared pan. Bake for about 40 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire rack and cool completely.

5. **Make frosting:** Beat butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and 5ml vanilla together until smooth and creamy. Stir in chopped pecans.

6. **Finish:** Frost the completely cooled cake. Serve and enjoy!

## Notes:
- Greek yogurt creates a slightly denser, more moist cake than oil
- Reduced sugar and increased yogurt makes this a healthier version
- The cake will have a subtle tangy flavor from the yogurt
- Should still be plenty sweet thanks to the natural carrot sweetness
