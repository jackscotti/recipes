# Shortcut Sangak (Persian Flatbread)

A quick, weeknight-friendly take on sangak — the Persian stone-baked flatbread —
made with a yogurt-enriched dough and pan-fried instead of oven-baked. Flavoured
with za'atar and finished with nigella seeds.

**Prep time:** 1 hour 45 minutes
**Cook time:** 10 minutes
**Servings:** 16 flatbreads
**Cuisine:** Persian
**Meal type:** Lunch
**Dietary:** Vegetarian
**Cooking method:** Pan-fry

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup warm water
- 1 tsp instant yeast
- 1 tbsp za'atar
- 1 tbsp olive oil (EVOO)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 scoop Greek yogurt
- 500g flour
- Nigella seeds, for topping

---

## Method

1. Mix the warm water and instant yeast together.

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, za'atar, olive oil, salt, and
   Greek yogurt.

3. Add the yeast mixture to the bowl and mix until a dough forms.

4. Let the dough rest for 90 minutes.

5. Punch the dough down to release air.

6. Divide the dough into 16 equal portions.

7. Stretch each portion of dough using extra olive oil (EVOO).

8. Cook each flatbread in a pan on high heat until charred on both sides.

9. Sprinkle nigella seeds on top before serving.
