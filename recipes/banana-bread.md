# Healthy Banana Bread (Sugar-Free Version)

A moist, delicious banana bread using Greek yoghurt instead of sugar. The ripe bananas provide natural sweetness, while the yoghurt adds moisture and a subtle tang.

**Prep time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook time:** 38–50 minutes  
**Servings:** 12 slices

---

## Ingredients

- 4 ripe bananas (about 320g, mashed)
- 1 large egg
- 5g vanilla extract
- 80g full-fat Greek yoghurt
- 2g ground cinnamon
- 170g plain flour
- 4.5g baking powder
- 5g bicarbonate of soda (natron)
- 3g salt
- 28g unsalted butter, melted
- Optional: 20g honey

---

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a standard 9×5" loaf tin and line the bottom with baking parchment.

2. Mash the bananas in a mixing bowl. Add the egg, vanilla, Greek yoghurt, cinnamon (and honey if using) and stir well to combine.

3. In a separate bowl, stir together the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, and salt. Add to the banana mixture and stir until just combined — don't overmix.

4. Gently fold in the melted butter.

5. Pour into the prepared tin and bake for 38–50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean. Check from 35 minutes onwards.

6. Cool in the tin for 5–10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

---

## Storage

- **Room temperature:** 3–5 days
- **Freezer:** Up to 3 months (wrap tightly once fully cooled)

---

## Notes

- The riper the bananas, the sweeter the bread — look for bananas with plenty of brown spots.
- To ripen bananas quickly, bake them (peel on) at 180°C for 6–8 minutes until the skin darkens.
- Optional mix-ins: 75g chopped walnuts, chocolate chips, or raisins.
